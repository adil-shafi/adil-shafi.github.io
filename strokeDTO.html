Stroke Disease Datatabel (Original)

This stroke data was perfect for integrating into the predictor environment since it already had multiple similar attributes to the other predictors. Hypertension, heart disease, blood glucose level and smoking status were a good place to start incorporating multiple overlapping probabilities. What was particularly interesting is the table having heart disease as an attribute. On top of this, there were interesting factors that stood out such as type of residency and marriage status which are perfect factors the average person is able to answer.
