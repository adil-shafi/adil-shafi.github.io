Chronic Kidney Disease Datatable (Modified)

This was a great piece of research that works well with the predictor model. The Chronic Kidney Disease dataset has only been used once in the MDP model as it was the last predictor to be implemented. It was found in the later stages towards the end of the project for the main reasons being that it had similar attributes and also that research showed that there is a link between heart disease, stroke, diabetes and chronic kidney disease. So it was the perfect fit for the predictor. It had a lot of interesting parameters that users are most likely to know such as alcohol intake, diet and sleep quality and many more. Only a handful of the attributes were selected to not make the input form too long.

Modifications made to the dataset

Modifications made to this dataset include changing the binary gender variables as well as the smoking input. Changing 0 and 1 to Male and Female and yes and no.
